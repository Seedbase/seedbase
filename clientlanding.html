What kind of Projects can I post?

Any kind of idea, project or business can be done. These include design services, software designs or Hard product prototyping. Our makers and creators Handle projects with creativity and brilliant imagination.
Requiring either;

  • Individual or team-based
  • Milestone set project
  • Retainer term project

How do I hire the right freelancer or find the right resident?

Create a client profile and submit a clear job post (for Freelancer and clear mentorship request (for resident).
Ensure to clearly specify the skill required and location (if Applicable) within application. More for finding resident,clearly state the stage and duration of advice required.

Can I Carry Out Project with a Team?

Yes you can select and hire more than one freelancer to carry out a project. Select and Hire freelancers who have applied for your particular job post. Negotiate with team and commence team collaboration upon initiating work Process.

How do payments work?

You can pay per milestones or by the 20/80 method. Milestones payment is done by which we remit payment.To the freelancer upon successful completion of a milestone.20/80 method is whereas a 20% payment is made upon commencement Of project and the remaining 80% is made upon successfully completing Project. Client is required to deposit full payment upon initiating contract.Payments are made through paypal gateway.

How Protected am I?

Our tool is easy to navigate and secured. Project expectations Are managed with signed contracts and built in project communication And engagement interphases. We ensure all milestones and projects are pre-approved by Clients before automated payments are made. Any dispute that may arise, we put project Or any payments on hold for a satisfactory resolution. Upon any disengagement of a project, For 20/80 method, 60% of balance fee is refunded to client. For milestones, further payments. Is discontinued and balance fees, deducting our service charge, is paid back to Client.
